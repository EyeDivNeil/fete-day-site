A creative production company producing arts, cultural and heritage projects across the UK. Fete Day works with artists, production teams, historians and scientists to bring to life outstanding and innovative projects – festivals, outdoor performances, exhibitions, media projects and engagement.

Latest news

Exciting projects in stunning venues. BBC Lincoln 16 includes both a civic commemoration outdoor event on the 100th anniversary of the Battle of the Somme at Lincoln Castle, and a newly commissioned outdoor performance Green Fields Beyond, with processions and large scale community performance in central Lincoln on 15th and 16th July 2016.

At Oxford Castle we are displaying the outstanding work of a group of young volunteers who have taken part in six months of research and photographic skills training to produce the outdoor exhibition Through the Lens.

Fete Day is also supporting the Story Museum, Oxford with the production of Alice’s Day on 4 July 2016
